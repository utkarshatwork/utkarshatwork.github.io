## Projects

### DebtStream: Digital Collections Analysis

An end-to-end analytics project for a B2B SaaS fintech platform analysing 10,000 customer accounts to optimise a digital collections funnel, evaluate an SMS A/B test, and identify recovery risk segments across client portfolios.

**Tools:** BigQuery SQL · Python · Power BI

**Key outcomes:** Identified a 62.19% drop-off at the digital journey entry point as the primary conversion failure. Confirmed that supportive SMS messaging (Variant) outperformed a direct payment reminder (Control) with a statistically significant +4.39pp plan setup lift and +£6.40 average amount collected per customer. Affordability completion shown to improve first instalment paid rate by 11pp and reduce broken-plan rate by 12pp.

[View Repository](https://github.com/utkarshatwork/DebtStream-Digital-Collections-Analysis)

---

### Argos: E-Commerce Marketing Analysis

A complete end-to-end e-commerce marketing analysis for Argos, a UK-based retailer, covering 4.5 years of transactional and behavioural data across funnel and conversion analysis, cohort retention, and RFM customer segmentation.

**Tools:** MySQL · Python · Power BI

**Key outcomes:** Identified 58.95% cart abandonment as the single largest revenue leakage point, with Tablet and Email as the worst performing combination at 63.28%. Cannot Lose Them segment (738 customers, £366 average revenue, 1,072 days absent) identified as the highest-ROI reactivation opportunity. Champions and Loyal Customers generate 57.03% of total revenue from just 34.84% of the customer base.

[View Repository](https://github.com/utkarshatwork/argos-ecommerce-marketing-analysis)

---

### Taylor and Francis: Marketing ROI Audit

An end-to-end marketing ROI audit for a global academic publishing firm, implementing a last-touch attribution model in SQL to evaluate the commercial return on a £650,000 marketing investment across four channels and twenty campaigns, with K-Means clustering for audience segmentation.

**Tools:** MySQL · Python · Power BI

**Key outcomes:** Identified a critical budget misallocation: Social Ads consumed 62% of total budget at a 0.53x ROAS (net loss of £187,000), while Email delivered a 28.57x ROAS on just 7.7% of budget. Corporate Pharma Research Scientists identified as the single most commercially valuable author segment at £677,500 in attributed revenue.

[View Repository](https://github.com/utkarshatwork/taylor-francis-marketing-analytics)

## How to Use This Template

If you want to create your own portfolio from this template, follow the four steps below.

### 1. Create a New Repository from this Template

Click the green **Use this template** button at the top of this repository page, then select **Create a new repository**.

### 2. Name Your Repository for GitHub Pages

To turn your portfolio into a live website, you must name your new repository in the following format:

`your-username.github.io`

Replace `your-username` with your actual GitHub username.

### 3. Customise Your Portfolio

Open the `index.html` file and personalise it. The file contains helpful `<!--UPDATE: ... -->` comments to guide you on where to change:

- Your name and title
- Social media links
- The About Me section
- Your project titles, descriptions and links
- Your list of skills

### 4. Add Your Files

- Upload your profile photo and project preview images to the `assets/images/` folder
- Upload your CV in PDF format to the `assets/` folder
- Create your favicon and add it to the root folder

Once you commit your changes, your portfolio website will be live at `https://your-username.github.io` within a few minutes.
