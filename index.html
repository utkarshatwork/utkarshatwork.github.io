About Me

I am a data analyst specialising in transforming complex datasets into clear, commercially actionable insights. I work across the full analytics stack, from data engineering and modelling in SQL, through statistical analysis and insight generation in Python, to interactive dashboards and data-driven narratives that help organisations make informed decisions.

With experience across multiple industries including SaaS Fintech, E-commerce, Ed-Tech, and Publishing, I have delivered analytics work spanning debt collections optimisation, marketing performance analysis, ROI audit, and product analytics.
